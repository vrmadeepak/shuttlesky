Types of Elevators we manufacture:



1. Passenger Elevator: Passenger elevators are simply made to move people from one point to another in a building. These elevators can have a capacity of 4-26 passengers.
2. Goods Elevator: These elevators are designed to carry loads in industry, building, hotel, car workshop, etc. These elevators are created depending on the type and weight of the product that the prospect is willing to load.
3. Automobile/Car Elevators: As the name suggests, these elevators are aimed to move cars and vehicles from one floor to another. We offer all types of vehicle elevators from 1500 kg for smaller cars to 5000 kg for bigger cars like SUVs and tempos.
4. Hospital Elevators: Hospital elevators are made to be installed to transport patients on wheelchairs or stretcher without disturbing his life support system along with the doctors and nurses. Special attention is given to the smooth, swift, and silent moment of the elevators.
5. Home Elevators: The home elevators are a great way to ferry vertically in multi-story homes and bungalows. These lifts are enclosed in a shaft and can travel vertically as much as 15 m.
