Since 2008

Shuttle Sky Lifts is the leading provider of elevator services to hospitals, DDA, homes, and industries.

Our company was founded in 2008 as a local enterprise providing various elevator services for homes, and other industrial companies. We focus on long-term and sustainable development concepts.

Our Mission

We provide the highest-quality end products to our customers.

Who We Are

We are proud to say that Shuttle Sky Lifts is a part of very dynamic and fast growing global elevator market in manufcaturing & installation of a variety of lifts. Supervised by professionals, Shuttle Sky Lifts has served in the elevator industry over a decade. The team having worked for several years with multinational companies are Highly Quality and Safety Concious and dedicated in providing the best products and services to their customers and ability to design and manufacture of special lifts based on various site conditions.

With a variety of products available, Shuttle Sky Lifts offers an extensive range of elevators for home/residential, DDA, commercial and industrial use. We help our customers to accurately define the particular elevator system which they require and ensure their choice meets their exact specifications.We have substantial experience in specialised elevators, including elevators for hospitals and nursing homes, shopping centres and public buildings through to vehicle elevators and more.

Shuttle Sky Lifts are equiped with Safety Systems: Safety is the number one priority of Shuttle Sky Lifts which is our basic spirit to ensure safety is through controlled technology such as back-up solution, safety drive operation and more than 10 other safety devices.

Energy Effiicient

It becomes increasingly more vital to provide energy saving lifts for our clients,our energy saving lifts not only help the environment by using less energy, they also save our money.

  • Low Energy LED Lights.
  • Lifts are programed to go into hidernate/standby when demand is low.
